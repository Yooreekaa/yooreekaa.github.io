Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2025

Yooreekaa Games ("we", "us", or "our") is an indie game studio based in Melbourne, Australia. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website at yooreekaagames.com.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not directly collect any personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email when you visit our website.

2. External Links

Our website contains links to third-party platforms including YouTube, Twitter (X), Reddit, and Steam. Clicking these links may allow those services to collect data about you in accordance with their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies before using those services.

3. Cookies and Analytics

Our website does not use cookies, analytics tools, or trackers. We aim to keep your browsing experience private and secure.

4. Game-Related Data

If you purchase or play our games through platforms like Steam, those platforms may collect usage data or other information as part of their services. We do not access or store that data ourselves.

5. Email Contact

If you contact us via email at yooreekaa@mail.com, we will only use your email address to respond to your inquiry. We do not share or store your contact details beyond what is necessary to communicate with you.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access or request deletion of any personal information you voluntarily provide to us via email. Simply contact us to make such a request.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we’ll revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please email us at yooreekaa@mail.com.
