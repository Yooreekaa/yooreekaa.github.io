About

Passionate About Game Design

We’re an indie game development company based in Melbourne, Australia, focused on making games that get straight to the point. Yooreekaa Games started from a lifelong love of gaming and the urge to create the kinds of games we always wanted to play — ones that are fun, challenging, and a little bit weird. We believe in creativity, personality, and memorable gameplay — no gimmicks, no filler. Our games are packed with charm, designed to stand out, and proudly go against the grain. We don’t follow trends — we follow fun.
