About

Learn more about who we are and how we started making games

Passionate About Game Design

We’re an indie game studio based in Melbourne, Australia, focused on making games that get straight to the point. Yooreekaa started from a lifelong love of gaming and the urge to create the kinds of games we always wanted to play — ones that are fun, challenging, and a little bit weird. We believe in creativity, personality, and memorable gameplay — no gimmicks, no filler. Our games are packed with charm, designed to stand out, and proudly go against the grain. We don’t follow trends — we follow fun.
